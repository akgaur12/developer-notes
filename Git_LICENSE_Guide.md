# LICENSE in Git 📜

This document explains **what a LICENSE is**, **why it is important**, and the **most common LICENSE types**, in a simple and beginner‑friendly way.

---

## What is a LICENSE? 🤔

A **LICENSE** tells others **what they are allowed to do with your code**.

Think of your code as a toy 🧸:

* Can others use it?
* Can they copy it?
* Can they sell it?

The LICENSE answers all these questions.

---

## Why is LICENSE important? ⚠️

Without a LICENSE:

* ❌ Others legally **cannot use your code**
* ❌ Companies will avoid your project
* ❌ Open‑source contribution becomes risky

👉 **Always add a LICENSE file** to your repo.

---

## Where does LICENSE live? 📁

```
project/
 ├── LICENSE
 ├── README.md
 └── src/
```

No extension. Just `LICENSE`.

---

## Common LICENSE Types 🧾

### 1. MIT License ⭐ (Most Popular)

**Meaning:**

* Anyone can use, copy, modify, sell
* They must give credit

**Good for:**

* FastAPI projects
* ML & GenAI tools
* Startups

**One‑line:**

> "Do anything, just give credit"

---

### 2. Apache License 2.0 🛡️

**Meaning:**

* Same freedom as MIT
* Adds **patent protection**

**Good for:**

* Enterprise projects
* Company‑backed open source
* AI frameworks

**One‑line:**

> "Free to use, and no patent lawsuits"

---

### 3. GPL (GNU General Public License) [v2 / v3] 🔁

**Meaning:**

* If you modify the code
* You **must share your changes**

**Good for:**

* Community projects
* When you want everything to stay open

**One‑line:**

> "Share your changes too"

---

### 4. BSD License (2-clause / 3-clause) 🎈

**Meaning:**

* Very similar to MIT
* Few extra conditions

**Good for:**

* Academic projects
* Research software

**One‑line:**

> "Free and simple"

---

### 5. MPL (Mozilla Public License) ⚖️

**Meaning:**

* Only modified files must be shared
* Other files can stay closed

**Good for:**

* Mixed open + closed source projects

**One‑line:**

> "Share what you change"

---

### 6. Proprietary License 🔒

**Meaning:**

* No one can use the code without permission

**Good for:**

* Closed‑source products
* Internal company software

**One‑line:**

> "Mine only"

---

## Quick Comparison Table 📊

| License     | Commercial Use | Share Changes | Patent Protection |
| ----------- | -------------- | ------------- | ----------------- |
| MIT         | ✅              | ❌             | ❌                 |
| Apache 2.0  | ✅              | ❌             | ✅                 |
| GPL         | ✅              | ✅             | ❌                 |
| BSD         | ✅              | ❌             | ❌                 |
| MPL         | ✅              | Partial       | ❌                 |
| Proprietary | ❌              | ❌             | ❌                 |

---

## Which LICENSE should I choose? 🎯

### Recommended for most devs:

* **MIT** → Simple & open
* **Apache 2.0** → Company‑safe

### For your stack (FastAPI / ML / GenAI):

> ✅ **MIT** or **Apache 2.0**

---

## One‑line takeaway 🧠

> **LICENSE defines how others can legally use your code.**
